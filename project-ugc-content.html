Project Overview

Proposed and led UGC engagement programs; crafted integrated social calendars; facilitated UGC delivery from briefing to content release and performance tracking.

Achieved consistent monthly delivery of 4 UGC content pieces, developed comprehensive UGC content style and content guidelines, and significantly improved UGC workflows for enhanced operational efficiency. The systematic approach to content management and performance tracking enabled data-driven optimization and established scalable processes for ongoing content production.
